How EV is calculated
EV = (true_prob × payout_multiplier) - (1 - true_prob)
Positive EV = bet has long-run mathematical value
Negative EV = house has the edge on this specific bet
Example: -110 bet at 55% true prob
(0.55 × 0.909) - (0.45) = +0.050
= +5.0% EV per dollar — worth betting
Example: -110 bet at 50% true prob
(0.50 × 0.909) - (0.50) = -0.045
= -4.5% EV per dollar — book has edge
SGP correlation adjustment
Positively correlated legs → true joint prob
HIGHER than independent math suggests.
That gap is where SGP edge comes from.
